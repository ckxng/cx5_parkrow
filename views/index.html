Services and Activities

Our Sunday morning services are a mix of traditional and contemporary worship styles. Our members come together as a family each Sunday morning to worship God with all our hearts, minds, and souls. We are open to the Holy Spirit moving us during the service to become more like Christ. So come as you are for you welcome here!! Oh, and bring your smile!!

During our worship service, please fill out a Guest card and place it in the offering tray when it is passed during the communion portion of our Worship. We would love to know that you visited and want to know how we can answer any questions that you may have or how we can pray for you.
