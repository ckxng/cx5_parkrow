Who We Are

The Primetimers Group at Park Row consists of anyone 55 and older who wishes to meet on a monthly basis to gt to know one another better. It affords us an opportunity to learn about any needs that exist in this group and to provide help when necessary. We meet in the Park Row Fellowship hall. Contact Georgia Cunningham through the church office, or simply join us at any or all of these times. Great folks... Great times!
